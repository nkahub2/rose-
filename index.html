HAPPY ROSE DAY MY LOVE

AYONA NITHUL



To the most beautiful rose in my life, I wish you a happy Rose Day, my love. We will share our love and roses until the end of time.




A poem for my Boo

"To the fairest bloom in my life's sweet garden,

On this rose day, my love, my heart's pardon.

With each petal's kiss, our love shall thrive,

Entwined with roses, until we cease to strive."
